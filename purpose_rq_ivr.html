Study Purpose
🎯 Purpose of the Study
Micro-Level Behavioral Change
Following In-VR Feedback
  • Investigating how rapidly, how profoundly, and for whom feedback reshapes behavioral pathways in IVR training
  • Presents the first design-oriented micro-window model of feedback dynamics in IVR
  • Yields practical timing principles for adaptive feedback system design
Research Questions
Four Guiding Research Questions
❓
Research
Questions
RQ 1 Behavioral Timing
How does immediate in-VR feedback influence behavioral strategies within a 30–60 sec window?
RQ 2 Predictive Power
Do behavioral shifts & entropy changes predict decision quality improvements post-feedback?
RQ 3 Learner Profiles
Can distinct learner profiles be identified based on feedback responsiveness and strategic shift patterns?
RQ 4 Individual Variation
How do feedback effects on task success & error reduction vary across learner types?
