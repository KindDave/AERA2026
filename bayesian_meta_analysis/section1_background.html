🏗️ Background & Purpose

Research Context — VR for Construction Safety Training

⚠️
1 · The Problem
Construction is One of the Most Hazardous Industries
  • Consistently high rates of fatal & non-fatal workplace injuries
  • Complex worksites demand rapid hazard recognition
📋
2 · The Gap
Traditional Training Provides Limited Support
  • Insufficient hazard recognition training
  • Low support for real-world decision-making
🥽
3 · The Solution
VR Offers Immersive, Risk-Free Training Environments
  • Simulates real hazards without physical risk
  • Supports active, embodied decision-making practice
🎯
4 · Study Objective
Evaluate VR Training Effectiveness vs. Traditional Instruction

To determine whether VR-based safety training produces significantly better learning outcomes than traditional or non-immersive instruction using Bayesian meta-analysis.
